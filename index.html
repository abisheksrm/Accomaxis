About Accomaxis

Accomax solutions is a US based health care focused IT company with offices in USA, Ireland and India. Our talented team will help to you improve employee engagement and productivity. The current solutions are designed to provide faster training and deployment of medical equipment, mandatory trainings and adherence.

Our Products
